PLAUSTRELLA

Rules Change

Stands equipped with portable anti-mounted barriers are designated in the army lists. All such designated troops fight as follows:

  • Fight as Heavy Foot in all respects except as noted below. This includes the Ghurid troops who would normally be Light Spear if not equipped with karwah.
  • Are based on square bases (same as Horde) with the troops and mobile barriers modeled appropriately on the base.
  • Have a movement rate of 2mu.
  • Receive a +2 modifier in close combat against mounted. This modifier does not apply if they are attacked in the flank or rear.
  • Receive a +1 modifier defending in ranged combat. This modifier does not apply when shot from behind.
  • Are Shattered if beaten by Elephants.

Timing

The choice to use this battle card is made when the stand is deployed.

Cost

+1 point per stand.
