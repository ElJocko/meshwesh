PREPARED DEFENSES

Rules Change

This battle card allows the player to deploy straight linear obstacles, 2mu x 1mu, that count as difficult terrain in all respects. Such obstacles must be deployed as follows:

  • They are deployed as part of the Center Deployment (rule 26), with the same restrictions as any Center Deployment troop stands.
  • They must be deployed in open terrain
  • Obstacles may be deployed in side edge and front corner contact with other obstacles. If gaps exist between obstacles, such gaps must be at least 4mu. This is intended to prevent artificially extending the line of obstacles by leaving small gaps between obstacles.
  • They are fixed in place and cannot be moved during the battle. In essence, they are strips of player-placed difficult terrain.

Numbers of individual obstacles allowed are described in the army list for each army.

Timing

This battle card is used when Center Deployment troops are placed.
