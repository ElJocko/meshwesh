SEPARATED VALETS

Rules Change

Two stands of Knights that can dismount as Elite Foot (Burgundian mixed men-at-arms and valets) are replaced for this battle with one stand of Elite Foot (Burgundian men-at-arms fighting dismounted) and one stand of Javelin Cavalry (Burgundian valets deployed separately from their normal use).

Timing

The choice to use this ability is made at deployment.

Cost

None.

This is a free army ability, so long as the other conditions are met.
