CHAINED TOGETHER

Rules Change

Bad Horse stands with this battle card gain the ability to attack enemy stands in ranged combat as follows:

  • They have an offensive missile combat factor of +2.
  • They may not attack using distant shooting if they moved that turn. They may attack in the enemy’s turn, or if they did not move on their own turn.
  • All ranged combat rules apply as normal.

In addition to the above (identical to the Shower Shooting battle card), they suffer the following penalties and gain the following abilities as a result of their chained formation:

  • Movement rate of 5mu (-1mu reduction from normal Bad Horse).
  • +1 command point to move (as rule 29.3.a).
  • +2 combat factor modification against Knights.
  • +1 combat factor modification against Javelin Cavalry.
  • -1 combat factor modification against Rabble or Skirmishers.
  • Do not follow up if the enemy is doubled in close combat (like War Wagons, rule 74.2)

Timing

This battle card has a continuous effect throughout the battle.

Cost

+0 point per stand.

This is an “all or none” ability.
