CHARGE THROUGH

Rules Change

A group of the mounted stand type designated in the army list may move through a group of the designated close-order foot type as if those foot were open order.

Timing

The choice to use this ability is made in the friendly movement phase.

This is an event card, usable once only; flip the card upside down to mark that it is no longer available for use in the current battle.

Cards are expended when played. The effect ends after the group move and has no impact on pass-through, fall-back, or any other interpenetration before or after they are played.

Cost

1 point per battle card (2 cards maximum).
